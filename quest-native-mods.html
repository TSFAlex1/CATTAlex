Quest-native Beat Saber modding — ModsBeforeFriday (MBF)

This guide explains how to mod Beat Saber natively on Meta Quest using https://mbf.bsquest.xyz. MBF is the modern, recommended method for Quest modding and replaces older workflows like BMBF for supported versions.

Important warnings

  • Modding modifies Beat Saber on your headset and can break when the game updates.
  • Only use official links — fake mod sites can brick your install.
  • Disable Beat Saber auto-updates to avoid losing mods.
  • Proceed at your own risk — modding is not officially supported by Meta.

Prerequisites

  1. Meta Quest 2 or Quest 3
  2. Beat Saber installed on the headset (compatible version)
  3. A phone or PC with a web browser
  4. Developer Mode enabled on your Quest
  5. A stable Wi-Fi connection

Overview (what you’ll do)

  1. Enable Developer Mode on your Quest
  2. Open ModsBeforeFriday.xyz
  3. Connect your Quest through the browser-based installer
  4. Patch Beat Saber automatically
  5. Install Quest-native mods and custom songs

Step-by-step instructions

1) Enable Developer Mode

  1. Open the Meta Quest mobile app.
  2. Select your headset → Settings → Developer Mode.
  3. Turn Developer Mode on (create a free dev organization if prompted).

2) Open ModsBeforeFriday

  1. On your PC or phone, go to https://mbf.bsquest.xyz.
  2. Read the compatibility notes shown on the site.

3) Connect your Quest

  1. Plug your Quest into your PC using a USB cable.
  2. Put on the headset and allow USB debugging when prompted.
  3. Return to the browser and continue once the device is detected.

4) Patch Beat Saber

  1. Click the patch/install button on ModsBeforeFriday.
  2. The site will automatically patch Beat Saber for mod support.
  3. Wait until the process fully completes — do not unplug early.

5) Install mods & custom songs

  1. Use the ModsBeforeFriday interface to install Quest-native mods.
  2. Install core mods first, then optional gameplay or cosmetic mods.
  3. Custom songs can be added through supported song managers.

6) Launch and verify

  • Open Beat Saber on your Quest.
  • Confirm the mod menu appears and songs load correctly.

Troubleshooting

  • Quest not detected: confirm Developer Mode and USB debugging are enabled.
  • Patching failed: ensure your Beat Saber version is supported by MBF.
  • Mods not loading: install required core mods first.
  • Game crashes: remove recently added mods and retry.

Best practices

  • Disable Beat Saber auto-updates.
  • Only install Quest-native mods marked as compatible.
  • Re-check ModsBeforeFriday after game updates.
  • Join Quest modding communities for updates and fixes.
